#### Added

- New idle system independent of Quickshell, powered by [axctl](https://github.com/Axenide/axctl).
- Support for MiniMax in the assistant.
- Move to workspace (silent) in the binds.

#### Improvements

- Switched to Quickshell's stable version.
- Workspace icons now use the same method as the dock for fetching, resulting in fewer missing icons.

#### Fixes

- Files (.json) not being automatically created.
- `axctl` remaining running in background and failing to reconnect on Ambxst restart.
- `wlsunset` implementation.
- Dynamic model detection in the assistant.
